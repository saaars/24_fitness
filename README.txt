Product Definition:
---------------------
Fitness Evolved is a fitness web application that allows users to work on specific goals. 
Users can navigate through a unique user interface of the human anatomy that displays various tips to 
improve their mental and physical health, as well as their nutrition, based on the part of the body they select. 
It is meant to help those who want to take their health into their own hands and work at their desired pace.
